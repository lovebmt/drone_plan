# UAV Manufacturing Complex

Ho Chi Minh City, May 1, 2026

## I. Project Overview

- **Project name:** UAV Manufacturing Complex
- **Sector:** Multi-application civilian UAV manufacturing

### Target application areas

- Agriculture: spraying and monitoring
- Industry: inspection and mapping
- Rescue and firefighting
- Education and training

### Financial targets

- **Year 1 revenue:** USD 8 million
- **Growth target:** 20% per year
- **Break-even:** approximately 18-24 months

## II. Business Model

### 1. Core products

- Agricultural spraying UAVs with 10-30L capacity
- Surveillance UAVs with AI camera systems
- Industrial inspection UAVs
- Educational UAV kits for STEM programs

### 2. Revenue composition

| Revenue source | Share |
| --- | ---: |
| UAV sales | 60% |
| Services, maintenance, and training | 20% |
| Software, AI, and data services | 10% |
| Accessories and batteries | 10% |

## III. Factory Master Plan

### 1. Land requirement

- **Proposed land area:** 2-3 hectares
- **Preferred locations:** near Ho Chi Minh City, Binh Duong, or Long An
- **Logistics requirement:** close to ports and logistics hubs

### 2. Functional zones

#### Production zone

- UAV assembly line
- Indoor and outdoor flight testing area
- Quality control and calibration

#### R&D zone

- Electronics lab for PCB and embedded systems
- AI and computer vision lab
- Environmental testing lab

#### Logistics zone

- Component warehouse
- Finished goods warehouse

#### Office zone

- Sales and marketing
- Executive management

## IV. Capacity and Output

### Year 1 production target

- **Capacity:** approximately 1,200-1,500 UAVs per year
- **Average selling price:** USD 4,000-8,000 per UAV
- **Revenue target:**  
  1,200 UAVs x approximately USD 6,500 per unit = approximately USD 7.8 million

## V. Human Resources and Organization

#### 3. Business team: approximately 20-30 people
